WELCOME TO THE MANDEM MERITORSHIP AWARDS

"Janmabhumiḥ sadā mātā, śālā janani-rūpiṇī ।
Gaṇeśa-maṇḍamaiḥ kṛtyaṁ, sahāyaṁ ruciraṁ dhruvam ॥"

(జన్మభూమిః సదా మాతా, శాలా జనని-రూపిణీ ।
గణేశ-మండమైః కృత్యం, సహాయం రుచిరం ధ్రువమ్ ॥)

We carry the sweet memories and roots of ZPHS Nooliveedu, where we spent our childhood and grew up. The roof that shielded us and the soil that nurtured us will always be close to our hearts. This humble initiative is our small way of showing gratitude—built with love to inspire every coming generation from 2026 onwards.
