WELCOME TO THE MANDEM MERITORSHIP AWARDS

ZPHS Nooliveedu — Where Our Journey Began
Empowering Young Minds & Academic Achievers
Rooted in the Soil of Annamayya District
Inspiring Every Coming Generation From 2026
"Janmabhumiḥ sadā mātā, śālā janani-rūpiṇī ।
Gaṇeśa-maṇḍamaiḥ kṛtyaṁ, sahāyaṁ ruciraṁ dhruvam ॥"
(జన్మభూమిః సదా మాతా, శాలా జనని-రూపిణీ । గణేశ-మండమైః కృత్యం, సహాయం రుచిరం ధ్రువమ్ ॥)
Meaning: Birthplace is always a mother, the school is the embodiment of a mother. This delightful assistance is a true duty performed by Ganesha Mandam.

We carry the sweet memories and roots of ZPHS Nooliveedu in Annamayya district, where we spent our childhood and grew up. The roof that shielded us and the soil that nurtured us will always be close to our hearts. This humble initiative is our small way of showing gratitude—built with love to inspire every coming generation from 2026 onwards.
