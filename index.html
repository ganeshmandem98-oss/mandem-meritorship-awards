Welcome

Welcome to The Mandem Meritorship Awards, an independent educational initiative established to recognize, honor, and encourage the outstanding academic achievements of the students of ZPHS Nooliveedu.

We believe that recognizing excellence inspires future generations to dream bigger, work harder, and achieve greater success.

VISION & MISSION

Our Vision

To create a culture where academic excellence is celebrated and every student is motivated to pursue higher goals through recognition, encouragement, and inspiration.

Our Mission

  • Recognize SSC School Toppers every year
  • Encourage excellence in education
  • Inspire future generations
  • Promote equal opportunities
  • Build a proud legacy for ZPHS Nooliveedu
