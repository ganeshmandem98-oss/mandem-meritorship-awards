IN LOVING MEMORY

Late Smt. Chimpiri Aadilakshumamma Garu

The Mandem Meritorship Awards are proudly presented in loving memory of Late Smt. Chimpiri Aadilakshumamma Garu, whose values, kindness, and belief in education continue to inspire this initiative.

Her inspiration and commitment towards education remain the foundation behind encouraging young students to achieve excellence.
